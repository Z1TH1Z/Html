Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. The Shawshank Redemption

    • Year: 1994
    • Main Actors: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman, Bob Gunton
    • Brief Description: Two prisoners, Andy Dufresne and Red, form an unlikely friendship while serving life sentences at Shawshank State Penitentiary.

  2. The Godfather

    • Year: 1972
    • Main Actors: Marlon Brando, Al Pacino, James Caan
    • Brief Description: The story of the Corleone family and their rise to power in the world of organized crime, as seen through the eyes of Michael Corleone.

  3. The Dark Knight

    • Year: 2008
    • Main Actors: Christian Bale, Heath Ledger, Aaron Eckhart
    • Brief Description: Batman must face off against the Joker, a chaotic force that threatens to destroy Gotham City, while also dealing with his own personal demons.

  4. 12 Angry Men

    • Year: 1957
    • Main Actors: Henry Fonda, E.G. Marshall, Jack Warden
    • Brief Description: A jury of 12 men must deliberate the fate of a young man accused of murder, exploring the complexities of justice and morality.

  5. The Silence of the Lambs

    • Year: 1991
    • Main Actors: Jodie Foster, Anthony Hopkins, Scott Glenn
    • Brief Description: FBI trainee Clarice Starling is assigned to investigate a series of gruesome murders, seeking the advice of imprisoned serial killer Hannibal Lecter.
